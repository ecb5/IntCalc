Task 2.14.a.

Suppose \(f^\prime(x)\neq{}f(x)\) Show that if
\begin{equation*} \left(f\cdot g\right)^\prime(x) = f^\prime(x)\cdot g^\prime(x) \end{equation*}
then
\begin{equation} g(x)=\exp\left({\int{\frac{1}{1-\frac{f(x)}{f^\prime(x)}}}\dx{x}{}}\right)\tag{2.4} \end{equation}
(Don’t let this formula (2.4) frighten you. Recall that \(\exp(x)=e^x\text{.}\))
