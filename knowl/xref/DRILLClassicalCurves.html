Problem 4.51.

Compute \(\dx{y}\) for each of the following. (You will see these formulas again in Drill 5.14.)

(a)

\(x^\frac23+y^\frac23 = 4\)

(b)

\(y^2=x^3-x+1\)

(c)

\(\left(x^2+y^2\right)^2+4x(x^2+y^2)=4y^2\)

(d)

\((y-2)^2(x^2+y^2)=2y^2\)

(e)

\((x^2+y^2+1)^2-4x^2=\frac32\)

(f)

\(y^2\left(1-x^2\right)=\left(x^2+2y-1\right)^2\)

(g)

\(x^4=x^2+y^2\)

(h)

\((x^2+y^2)(y^2+x(x+4))=4xy^2\)
