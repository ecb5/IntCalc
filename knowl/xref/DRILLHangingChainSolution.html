Drill 1.3.0.4.

Show that
\begin{equation} y=\frac{H}{w} \left(\frac{e^{\frac{wx}{H}}+e^{-\frac{wx}{H}}}{2}\right)\tag{1.10} \end{equation}
solves equation (1.7).
Hint.
Notice that you are not asked to solve (1.7). You are only asked to show that \(y=\frac{H}{w} \left(\frac{e^{\frac{wx}{H}}+e^{-\frac{wx}{H}}}{2}\right)\) is a solution.
