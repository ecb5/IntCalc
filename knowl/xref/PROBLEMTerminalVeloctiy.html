Problem 3.1.0.2. Terminal Velocity.

(a)

Let \(M=m-m_w\) and note that \(M\gt0\) (why?). Show that IVP (3.1) can be re-expressed as
\begin{align*} \frac{\dx{v}}{Rv-Mg}=\frac{-1}{m}\dx{t},\amp{}\amp{} v(0)=v_0. \end{align*}

(b)

Use the substitution \(z=Rv-Mg\text{,}\) to rewrite the above equation as
\begin{equation} \frac{\frac{1}{R}\dx{z}}{z}=-\frac{1}{m}\dx{t}.\tag{3.2} \end{equation}
