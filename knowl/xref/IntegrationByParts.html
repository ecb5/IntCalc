Theorem 19.18. Integration by Parts.

  • Differential Form:
    \begin{equation} u\dx{v}{}=\dx(uv){}-v\dx{u}. \tag{19.13} \end{equation}
  • Functional Form:
    \begin{equation} \begin{split} \int{}u\dx{v}{}=\int{} \left(\dx(uv){}-v\dx{u}{}\right) =\amp{} \int\dx(uv){}-\int v\dx{u}\\ \amp{}=uv-\int v\dx{u}. \end{split}\tag{19.14} \end{equation}
