Problem 24.10.0.1.

For each of the following functions, find the power series expansion
\begin{equation*} f\left(x\right)=a_0+a_1\left(x-a\right)+a_2{\left(x-a\right)}^2+a_3{\left(x-a\right)}^3+\dots \end{equation*}
for the indicated value of \(a\) by manipulating a known series. [Make sure you write out enough terms so there is a discernible pattern.]
  1. \(\displaystyle f\left(x\right)={\ln (x)}, a=1\)
  2. \(\displaystyle f\left(x\right)=e^x , a=5\)
  3. \(\displaystyle f\left(x\right)=1+x+x^2 , a=-2\)
