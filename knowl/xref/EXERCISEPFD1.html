Problem 24.3.0.1.

Solve the original initial value problem
\begin{align*} \frac{\dx{y}}{y\left(100-y\right)}=.0001\dx{t},\amp{}\amp{} y(0)=y_0 \end{align*}
for the case \(0\lt{}y\lt{}100\text{.}\)
Answer.
\begin{equation*} y=\frac{100y_0}{y_0+\left(100-y_0\right)e^{-0.01t}} \end{equation*}
