Drill 4.47.

Compute \(\dx{y}\) for each of the following:
  1. \(\displaystyle y=\frac{x^2-2x+4}{x}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle y=x^2-\sqrt{x}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle y=\left(\sqrt[3]{x}\right)^2-\left(\sqrt{x}\right)^3\)
  4. \(\displaystyle y=\left(\frac{1}{x^2}+\frac{1}{x^3}\right)\left(x^2+x^3\right)\)
  5. \(\displaystyle y=\sqrt{3x^4-7x^3+3}\)
  6. \(\displaystyle y=\sqrt{1+\sqrt{1+\sqrt{1+x}}}\)
  7. \(\displaystyle y=\sqrt{x}+\sqrt{z}\)
  8. \(\displaystyle y=\sqrt{x+z}\)
  9. \(\displaystyle y=\sqrt{x z}\)
  10. \(c^2t^2-x^2-y^2-z^2=0\text{,}\) where \(c\) is a constant 15 .
