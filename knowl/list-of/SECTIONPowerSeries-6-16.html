Don’t forget that when you integrate, you need to determine the value of the arbitrary constant \(C\text{.}\) Also, there may be more than one way to obtain a series representation.

\(f\left(x\right)=\frac{1}{3+5x}\)
(b)
\(f\left(x\right)=\frac{1}{1-x^3}\)
(c)
\(f\left(x\right)=\ln(3+x) \)
(d)
\(f\left(x\right)=x^2{\ln}\left(1+x\right)\)
(e)
\(f\left(x\right)=x^2{\ln}\left(1+x\right)\)
(f)
\(f\left(x\right)={\ln}(1-x^2)\)
