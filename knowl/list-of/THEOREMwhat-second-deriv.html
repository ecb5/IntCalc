Theorem 9.6.1.7. Concavity.

  1. If \(\eval{\dfdxn{y}{x}{2}}{x=a}{}\gt 0\) on some open interval then \(\dfdx{y}{x}\) is increasing, and therefore the graph of \(y\) is concave upward, on the same interval.
