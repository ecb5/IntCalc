Problem 2.2.2.5.

Compute each integral by decomposing the integrand using any of the methods you’ve seen so far.
(a)
\(\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{x^2-9}\dx{x}\)
(b)
\(\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{x^2-4}\dx{x}\)
(c)
\(\displaystyle\int \frac{1}{x^2-13x+36 }\dx{x}\)
(d)
\(\displaystyle \int \frac{1}{x^4-13x^2+36 }\dx{x}\)
Hint.
Let \(y=x^2\) and use the results of parts (a), (b), and (c).
