Problem 21.2.2.7.

(a)
Show that
\begin{equation*} d\left(\sum^{n_1}_{n=n_0}{y_n\left(x\right)}\right)=\sum^{n_1}_{n=n_0}{{d(y}_n\left(x\right))} \end{equation*}
and
\begin{equation*} \int{\left(\sum^{n_1}_{n=n_0}{y_n\left(x\right)}\right)\dx{x}}=\sum^{n_1}_{n=n_0}{\int{y_n\left(x\right)}\dx{x}} \end{equation*}
(b)
Compute the following
  1. \(\displaystyle \dx\left(\sum^N_{n=0}{x^n}\right)\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \int{\left(\sum^N_{n=0}{x^n}\right)} \dx{x}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \int^{2\pi }_{x=0}\left(\sum^N_{n=1} \sin(nx)\right)\dx{x}\)
