Problem 19.2.2.3.
Despite our personal preference for dividing last there are legitimate reasons to divide first. For example show that the expression \(\frac{x}{x-1}\) cannot be decomposed by the “dividing last” method described in Example 19.2.2.1, but it can be decomposed by the “dividing first” method. Are we really “decomposing” the fraction in this case. Explain.
