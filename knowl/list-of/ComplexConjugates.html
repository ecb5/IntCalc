Theorem 2.2.4.8. Properties of the Complex Conjugate.
Let \(z=a+bi\) and \(w=c+di\text{,}\) then
(a)
\(\overline{z+w}=\overline{\ z\ }+~\overline{w}\)
(b)
\(\overline{z\cdot w}=\overline{\ z\ }\cdot \overline{w}\)
(c)
\(z+\overline{\ z\ }=2a\) which is a real number
(d)
\(z-\overline{\ z\ }=2bi\)
(e)
\(z\cdot \overline{\ z\ }=a^2+b^2\) which is a nonnegative real number.
