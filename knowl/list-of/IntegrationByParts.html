Theorem 2.3.0.6. Integration by Parts.

  • Differential Form:
    \begin{equation} \int{}u\dx{v} =uv-\int{}v\dx{u}\tag{2.59} \end{equation}
    .
  • Functional Form:
    \begin{equation} \int{}f(x)g^\prime(x)\dx{x} = f(x)\cdot g(x)-\int g(x)f^\prime(x) \dx{x}\tag{2.60} \end{equation}
    .
