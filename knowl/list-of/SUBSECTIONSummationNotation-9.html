Problem 4.2.2.3.

(a)
Show that the following are true.
\begin{equation*} \sum^b_{n=a}{\left(x_n+y_n\right)}=\sum^b_{n=a}{x_n}+\sum^b_{n=a}{y_n} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \sum^b_{n=a}{c\cdot y_n=c\sum^b_{n=a}{y_n}} \end{equation*}
where \(c\) is a constant. What arithmetic properties do these represent?
(b)
Is it true that \(\sum^b_{n=a}{(x_n\cdot y_n)}=\left(\sum^b_{n=a}{x_n}\right)\cdot\left(\sum^b_{n=a}{y_n}\right)?\)
