Problem 19.2.2.8.

By a similar argument, show that the HCUM will also compute the correct value of \(A\text{,}\) \(B\text{,}\) and \(C\) in the decomposition
\begin{equation*} \frac{1}{(x-a)(x-b)(x-c)}=\frac{A}{x-a}+\frac{B}{x-b}+\frac{C}{x-c}\text{.} \end{equation*}
It is straightforward to generalize this result to an arbitrary number of linear factors.
