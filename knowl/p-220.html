This sounds harder than it is. If we write
\begin{gather*} 121_{10} = a_0 +a_1\cdot5 + a_2\cdot5^2 \end{gather*}
we can find the unknown coefficients in the order given as follows. Divide each side of the above equation by \(5\text{.}\) On the right we get
\begin{gather*} a_1+a_2\cdot5, \text{ with a remainder of $a_0$.} \end{gather*}
On the left we get \(\frac{121}{5} = 24\) with a remainder of \(1\text{.}\) So \(a_0 = 1\) and
\begin{gather*} 24 = a_1+a_2\cdot5. \end{gather*}
