Definition 9.4.0.7. Local Minimum.

Suppose that \(l\) is a number in the domain of \(y(x)\text{.}\) Then \(y(l)\) is a local minimum of \(y\) if there is an open interval, \(I\text{,}\) such that \(y(l)\le y(x)\) for every \(x\) which is in both the interval \(I\) and the domain of \(y(x)\text{.}\)
