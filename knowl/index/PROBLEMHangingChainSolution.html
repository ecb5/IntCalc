Problem 2.2.0.4.

Show that \(y=\frac{H}{w} \left(\frac{e^{\frac{wx}{H}}+e^{-\frac{wx}{H}}}{2}\right)\) solves equation (2.6).
Hint.
Notice that you are not asked to solve (2.6). You are only asked to show that \(y=\frac{H}{w} \left(\frac{e^{\frac{wx}{H}}+e^{-\frac{wx}{H}}}{2}\right)\) is a solution.
