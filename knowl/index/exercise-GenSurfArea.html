Problem 19.144. General Surface Area.

Show that the surface area of the surface generated by revolving the following curve \(y=y(x)\) about the line \(x=c\) to the left of curve is given by
\begin{equation*} \int_{X=A}^B 2\pi(x-c)\dx{s} \text{.} \end{equation*}
What if the line was to the right of the curve? What if it passed through the curve?
