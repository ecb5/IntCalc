Definition 6.7.1.4. The Derivative of the Inverse Sine.

The derivative of the inverse sine function is defined to be,
\begin{equation*} \dfdx{(\inverse\sin(x))}{x}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}\text{,} \end{equation*}
where \(-\frac{\pi}{2}\lt \inverse\sin(x)\lt \frac{\pi}{2}\text{.}\)
