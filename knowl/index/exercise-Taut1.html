\begin{equation*} \int_{\theta =\theta_0}^{\pi }\frac{\dx{s}}{v}=\int_{\theta =\theta_0}^{\pi }\frac{\sin\left(\frac{\theta }{2}\right) }{v}\dx{\theta } \end{equation*}
