Problem 17.2.2.9.

(a)
Give a definition like Definition 17.2.2.2 for \(\rlimit{x}{a}{f(x) }=-\infty \)
(b)
Give a definition like Definition 17.2.2.7 for \(\rlimit{x}{a}{f(x) }=-\infty \)
(c)
Use your definitions in parts (a) and (b) to prove that \(\llimit{x}{a}{f(x) }=-\infty \) if and only if \(\llimit{x}{a}{\left(-f\left(x\right)\right) }=\infty \)
