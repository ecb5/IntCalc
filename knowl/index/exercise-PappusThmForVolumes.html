Problem 2.167. Pappus Theorem of Volumes.

Use cylindrical shells to show that the volume of the solid generated by revolving this region about the line \(x=0\) is given by
\begin{equation*} 2\pi \bar{x}_R\cdot (\text{area of }R)\text{.} \end{equation*}
This is Pappus’ Theorem for volumes.
