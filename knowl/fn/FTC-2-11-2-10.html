Footnote 1.5.

Sometimes we will call the line “the \(x\)-axis” but this is a bit confusing since we’ve already used \(x\) to indicate a position on the line. Using \(x\) in two incompatible ways like this is not precise enough for mathematics, but, for better or worse mathematics is done, and learned, by flawed and imprecise human creatures so we have to find ways to cope. We must be very careful about the meanings of our symbols.
