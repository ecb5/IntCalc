Thus
\begin{align*} \int\tan(x)\dx{x} = \int\frac{\sin(x)}{\cos(x)}\dx{x}\amp=\int\frac{\sin(x)}{z}\cdot\frac{-\dx{z}}{\sin(x)}\\ \amp=-\int\frac{\dx{z}}{z}\\ \amp=-\ln(z)+c\\ \amp=(-1)\ln(\cos(x))+c\\ \amp=\ln\left((\cos(x))^{-1}\right)+c\\ \amp=\ln\left(\sec(x)\right)+c \end{align*}
