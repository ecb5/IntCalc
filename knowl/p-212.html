However, once the place value system is adopted we do lose some flexibility, since the digits must appear in the proper order. For example "\(72534\)" is a completely different number than "\(43527\text{,}\)" but
\begin{gather*} 4\cdot10^4+3\cdot10^3+5\cdot10^2+2\cdot10^1+7\cdot10^0 \end{gather*}
and
\begin{gather*} 7\cdot10^0+2\cdot10^1+5\cdot10^2+3\cdot10^3+4\cdot10^4 \end{gather*}
are the same. It will be convenient for us to use this last ordering soon.
